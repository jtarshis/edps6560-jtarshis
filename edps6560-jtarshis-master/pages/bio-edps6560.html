About Me


I'm a learning and development professional currently residing in Salt Lake City, Utah, USA.


I hold a Bachelor's in Music Education from the University of Utah, where I am also pursuing a Master's of Educational Psychology, as part of the Instructional Design and Educational Technology Program. My work experience includes a variety of training and facilitation experiences. My primary focus has been in training internal employees in New Hire and Onboarding classrooms. I also have experience training external clients in both retail environments and in software installation.

In my spare time, I am an avid guitarist, staying active by playing in bands and small projects. I also enjoy video games, root for the New England Patriots, regularly attend shows and live music, and in the summer I spend my weekends golfing!

I have two pets, a dog named Odin and a cat named Sif.
